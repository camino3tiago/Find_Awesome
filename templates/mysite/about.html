About

You can share your experiences, memories with people from all over the world!!

We all are travellers. Even if it feels like a trivial daily scene to you, it will be possibly different to people with other cultural backgrounds.

Through living in three countries, I had some cross-cultural experiences. Some cultures seemed interesting and great, but some seemed shocked and unacceptable. These experiences helped me to see my cultures objectively.

I'm Japanese, and when I stayed in Mexico, I was surprised at the difference in the sense of time. In my country, trains and buses run almost on time, but they don't there. When I started my stay, I was always frustrated. However as I gradually got used to life and got along with the locals, I began to think like this...

"If the bus comes on time, I'm lucky today!!"
In other words, I changed my mind positively and didn't take things for granted.

Of course, we don't think exactly the same, even if they have the same roots. So I often felt a difference in thinking even though I'm in Japan.
But in order to understand each other, we should try to know the other fist.

Communicating your thoughts and feelings leads to create or at least to make closer to the world where everyone can understand each other.

So please share your experiences here and find something awesome too!!
